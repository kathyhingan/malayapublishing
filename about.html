Our story

We're named after freedom. And after joy.

Malaya Publishing exists for one reason: to help Filipino business owners win back the freedom they started their business for, so they can actually live the life that freedom is supposed to buy. That's not a slogan for us. It's the names of our two daughters.

How it started

It began when I found out I was pregnant.

I was the SEO manager for an Australian agency. Good work, good clients. But when I found out I was pregnant with our firstborn, something became very clear to me.

Every hour I gave that agency was an hour I was trading away, and the asset I was building with those hours would never be mine. It would be theirs. I was pouring my best years into someone else's business, and I would never get that time back with my daughter.

So I made a decision. I would build my own. I would have my own clients, on my own terms, with my own time. That was the beginning of Malaya Publishing.

The freedom I wanted for myself is the freedom we now build for other owners.
