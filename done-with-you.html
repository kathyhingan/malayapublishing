Fair questions

What owners ask us first.

Bakit may discovery call bago ang price?

Because prescribing without diagnosing is malpractice. A clinic with one branch and a supplier with five branches need very different things. The free Growth Audit lets us give you a real number for your actual business, not a guess. And you keep the plan whether or not you hire us.

Ano ang mangyayari pagkatapos ng 90 days? Iiwan niyo ba ako?

You'll own the systems and your team will know how to run them, that's the design. Many owners choose to keep us on for ongoing work (that's Done-For-You), but you're never trapped. Independence is the promise, not a monthly bill you can't escape.

Kaya ba ito ng team ko?

Yes. We build it with them watching and train them until they're confident. We don't hand you a black box. If they can use Messenger, they can run what we build.

Magkano talaga, mahigit ₱25,497?

₱25,497 is the starting point. The final number depends on the factors above: locations, team, systems, and where you're starting. The audit gives you the exact figure before you commit.
