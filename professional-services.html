Will this feel too "salesy" for my profession?

No. We know a law firm or an accounting practice can't market like a retail shop. Everything we build fits a professional standard: measured, credible, and focused on being found and trusted by the right clients, not on loud promotion.

I'm bound by professional conduct and confidentiality rules. Does that work?

Yes. We build within your profession's advertising and confidentiality rules, and we defer to your judgment on what's appropriate. The systems handle process and response, and you stay in control of anything client-facing.

I don't have time to create content or run a podcast.

That's the point of Done-With-You and Done-For-You. We can build and run the authority side for you, using your expertise but not your hours, or set it up so a small amount of your time goes a long way. You decide how hands-on to be.
